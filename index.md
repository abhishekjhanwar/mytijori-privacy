# Privacy Policy — MyTijori

**Last updated: 9 March 2026**

## Overview

MyTijori is an offline password vault. It stores all data locally on your device. This app does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect or share user data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No advertising identifiers
- No cookies or tracking

To the best of our knowledge, MyTijori does not cause any user data to be collected or transmitted. However, the underlying operating system or device manufacturer may independently collect system-level diagnostics or crash information outside of our control.

## Data Storage

- All entry content (passwords, usernames) is encrypted and stored locally on your device
- Your master password never leaves your device
- No data is sent to any server, cloud, or third party

## Data Deletion

Because no data is collected or transmitted to any servers, there is no account or personal data for us to delete. You can permanently delete all your MyTijori data at any time by simply uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

## Backups

Backups are created locally on your device and encrypted with your master password. If you choose to share a backup file externally, that is your action — the app itself does not upload anything.

## Permissions

MyTijori requests only:

- **Biometric hardware** — for optional fingerprint or face unlock

No internet, camera, contacts, location, or storage permissions are requested. Backups are handled through Android's built-in file picker, which does not require storage permissions.

## Third-Party Services

MyTijori uses no third-party services, SDKs, or analytics libraries.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the "last updated" date at the top will be revised.
